Customs Clearing & Forwarding

Customs clearance is the most sensitive and time-critical stage of the logistics process. Delays, documentation errors, or poor coordination at this stage can result in significant operational and financial losses, especially for time-sensitive and regulated cargo.

Jabal Logistics provides structured customs clearing and forwarding services designed to ensure efficient, compliant, and predictable cargo release across Tanzania’s ports and border points.


What We Do

Our clearing and forwarding services cover a wide range of cargo types, including general commercial goods, vehicles, machinery, project cargo, and perishable goods.

Our scope includes:

  • Customs declarations and clearance processing
  • Coordination with TRA and port authorities
  • Clearance of import, export, and transit cargo
  • Handling of time-sensitive and regulated shipments
  • Clearing and forwarding of perishable goods requiring expedited processing
  • Coordination of vehicle clearance within customs procedures

We act as the central coordination point between clients, customs authorities, and operational partners.

Handling of Perishable Goods

Perishable goods require speed, preparation, and procedural accuracy. Even minor delays can compromise cargo integrity and commercial value.

Our approach to perishable cargo focuses on:

  • Early documentation preparation and verification
  • Prioritization of clearance steps where applicable
  • Close coordination with port, customs, and inspection authorities
  • Alignment of clearance and onward transportation timelines

We do not treat perishable cargo as routine clearance. Each shipment is planned with urgency, care, and coordination to minimize dwell time at ports and border points.

How We Deliver

We apply a structured clearance workflow supported by early document review, continuous follow-up, and real-time coordination. For time-sensitive and perishable shipments, clearance steps are aligned with transportation readiness to reduce exposure to delays. Clients are kept informed throughout the process, with clear communication on status, requirements, and next steps.

Documents, Compliance & Authorities

This service involves coordination with:

  • Tanzania Revenue Authority (TRA)
  • Port and customs authorities
  • Relevant inspection and regulatory agencies

Typical Use Cases

  • Importers of food products and agricultural produce
  • Businesses handling temperature-sensitive cargo
  • NGOs and institutions managing relief or food-related shipments
  • Regional traders moving perishable goods through transit corridors

Why Choose Jabal for Customs Clearance

Because customs clearance is not just paperwork. It is risk management. We bring structure, urgency, and accountability into the clearing and forwarding process especially where time-sensitive and perishable cargo is involved.
