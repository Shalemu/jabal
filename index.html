About Us

Quick Transport and Logistics Solutions

Jabal Logistics Limited is a Tanzania-based logistics company providing structured, compliance-focused support in documentation, customs clearance, transportation, and end-to-end cargo coordination. We support businesses and organizations of different scales by bringing clarity, preparation, and accountability into logistics operations. Our role is to reduce delays, manage compliance requirements, and ensure cargo moves smoothly from port to final destination.

Structured & Compliant

We ensure all documentation and logistics processes are fully compliant with regulations, minimizing risk and delays.

Efficient & Reliable Delivery

Cargo is handled and transported efficiently, ensuring timely delivery from port to final destination.

Our Reach & Operational Footprint

Nationwide Coverage, Port-Centered Expertise

Jabal Logistics supports cargo operations through multiple ports and major land borders in Tanzania. Our presence includes Dar es Salaam, Tanga, Zanzibar, Pemba, and Mtwara ports, as well as high-volume trade corridors and supporting border posts connecting Tanzania to neighboring countries.

This reach enables us to coordinate compliant and reliable logistics solutions across different entry points and regional trade routes.
