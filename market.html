SCOPE & OPERATIONS

Markets & Operations

Nationwide Logistics Coverage Across Key Trade Corridors

Jabal Logistics Limited operates across Tanzania’s principal logistics corridors, supporting cargo movement through key ports and major land border points under full customs control.

Our operational footprint enables us to support import, export, and transit cargo across different regions, trade volumes, and regulatory environments with consistency and compliance.

Port Operations

We support cargo clearance and coordination through the following ports:

  • Dar es Salaam Port
  • Tanga Port
  • Zanzibar Port
  • Pemba Port
  • Mtwara Port

This multi-port presence allows flexibility in routing based on cargo type, destination, and operational efficiency.

Major Trade Borders

Key Land Trade Corridors in Tanzania

These are Tanzania’s primary land trade corridors where significant commercial and transit cargo moves daily under full TRA customs operations.

  • Tanzania – Zambia (Tunduma / Nakonde): A major regional trade corridor supporting transit to Zambia, Malawi, and the Democratic Republic of Congo.
  • Tanzania – Kenya (Namanga): A key trade and passenger hub operating as a One Stop Border Post (OSBP), widely used for East African Community (EAC) trade.
  • Tanzania – Rwanda (Rusumo): An OSBP serving Rwanda, Burundi, and eastern DRC, forming a critical regional transit corridor.
  • Tanzania – Malawi (Kasumulo / Songwe): A major trade route supporting Malawi and Mozambique, operating under full TRA customs control.
  • Tanzania – Uganda (Mutukula): An important EAC trade corridor supporting cargo transit to Uganda and South Sudan.
